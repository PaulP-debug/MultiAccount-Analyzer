    General principles

    Every multi-account case, at heart, comes back to these.
    • Each player is allowed one account only. We don't proactively hunt for duplicates — but once one surfaces, we act on it.
    • The goal: the player ends up with exactly one account, and dispute risk is minimized along the way.
    • Tell players plainly: one account per player, point them to the TOS clause if useful, and warn that repeat offenses can trigger an automatic system suspension.
    • We can only ever refund a player's Purchase amount — never the full balance, even if the balance is higher. Don't promise a full-balance refund.

    Can we even assist?

    A player must contact us from the account they need help with. Check this before doing anything else.
    • Can't log in, KYC not completed, no policy reason for the lockout: we cannot assist — even if there's a balance or other assets on the account. Purchase receipts alone don't prove ownership.
    • Can't log in, but KYC is completed: a Tier 2 agent can verify ownership via face authentication and support from there.
    • Can log in: player should contact us from that account directly — assist as usual.
